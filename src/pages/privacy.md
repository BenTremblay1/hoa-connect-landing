_Last updated_: February 24, 2026

HOA Connect collects only the information needed to run this waitlist and communicate with interested communities.

## Data We Collect

- Waitlist data you submit: email, optional role, optional HOA size.
- Marketing attribution data: optional `utm_source`, `utm_medium`, `utm_campaign` from URL parameters.
- Basic analytics events (via Plausible) to understand site usage.

## How We Use Data

- Confirm your waitlist signup.
- Send launch and beta-access updates.
- Improve site experience and communications.

## Services We Use

- **Supabase** for waitlist storage.
- **Resend** for transactional email delivery.
- **Plausible** for privacy-focused analytics.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with service providers needed to operate this waitlist and only for that purpose.

## Retention

We retain waitlist records as needed for launch communications, unless deletion is requested.

## Your Rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your waitlist data at any time.

## Contact

For privacy requests, contact: `privacy@hoaconnect.io`.
